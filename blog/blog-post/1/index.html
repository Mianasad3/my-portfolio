Badminton

Date: 20th March 2022

First of all, I would like to tell you that I am an international badminton player. I am the holder of numerous titles. Badminton depends upon techniques and physique. This game involves the movement of every muscle of your body, which makes you fit and decreases the level of ‘bad’ diseases in your body.

Badminton is based on following rules:

  1. 3 set game.
  2. Each set contains 21 points
  3. Each set has an interval after 11 points

Strategy

I am an attacking player, though attack requires a lot of arm force. Your muscles should be strong enough for attack; your jump, arm force and speed matters. With the speed you hit the smash, the defender will defend it at the same speed so your speed should be fast enough to pick up the shuttle. But the benefit of a strong smash is it doesn't allow the opposite player to defend. Smash should be placed on the right spot at the right time.

Highlighted areas in the image show where you can place your smash. Players will always be standing in the middle of the court, so if you place your smash in the corners it will be difficult for the opposite players to defend back.

To hit a power smash you need to follow the body language shown in the picture. Take your racket to extreme back; then match your time with shuttle; make a jump and smash the shuttle to the opponent's court.

Tips and Tricks

So here are some tips you need to get strong on.

  1. Improve your footwork.
  2. Improve your agility.
  3. Devise your game strategy.
  4. Learn advanced techniques.
  5. Improve your stamina.

By following these tips you can become a good player. Your brain should be active enough to place your shots and to confuse opponents. Your body language should scare your opponent which will make him lose confidence. In the end, I would like to elaborate a little bit more about the shots you can play according to your convenience.
